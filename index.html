Steve Irwin

  • Feb 22, 1962: Steve Irwin is born.
  • 1991: Steve Irwin takes over management of the Queensland Reptile and Fauna Park, which he renamed Australia Zoo in 1998.
  • June 4, 1992: Steve Irwin marries his wife, Terri Raines. They later had two children, Bindi and Robert.
  • 1996: The first episode of his show The Crocodile Hunter airs on Australian television, and on American television the following year.The show ran for 5 seasons up until 2004.
  • 1997: Steve Irwin discovers a new species of turtle while on a fishing trip with his father off the coast of Queensland. He named the turtle Irwin's Turtle (Elseya irwini).
  • Nov. 2003: Steve Irwin and his television crew suspended operations off the coast of the Baja California Peninsula to aid in a search and rescue of two divers. One of the divers was found alive.
  • Sept 4, 2006: Steve Irwin dies in a tragic accident involving a sting ray.
  • 2009: A new discovered species of land snail in Australia is named after Steve Irwin. The snail is called Crikey steveirwini.
